About Us

Enjoy Your Stay at Hotel Sky
A New Vision of Luxury Hotel

Step into a realm of absolute comfort and sophistication with our luxurious hotel rooms. Each bed is a sanctuary of plush pillows and silky sheets, meticulously designed to cradle you into a restful night's sleep.

Nestled in the heart of Cape Town, our hotel offers an enchanting escape where you can unwind and indulge in luxury. Whether you're enjoying a moonlit swim or relaxing by the poolside with a cocktail in hand, every moment at our night pool hotel is a delightful blend of elegance and tranquility, promising an unforgettable experience under the velvet night sky.

Who Are We:

Nestled in the heart of Sandton, Hotel Sky offers a luxurious escape amidst the vibrant pulse of the city. Located just steps away from Nelson Mandela Square, our hotel embodies the perfect blend of modern elegance and local charm. Hotel Sky was founded on the desire to make happiness, luxury, and indulgence as affordable and accessible as possible. At Hotel Sky, we don't just wish to provide a nice and affordable place to stay. Our vision is to provide every guest and visitor with an amazing and extraordinary experience. From our seamless app-based check-in and room access technology to our stunning design, we have spared no cost in making our hotels as unique and beautiful as possible. But we have not just invested in cutting-edge technology and stunning design; we have also invested in exceptional people. While facilities, technology, amenities, and indulgences are vital, what truly distinguishes Hotel Sky is our fantastic staff of committed and passionate experts. Individuals who embody the Hotel Sky dream. Your dream. Our reality.
