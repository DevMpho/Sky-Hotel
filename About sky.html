About Us

Who Are We

Nestled in the heart of Cape town, Sky Hotel offers a luxurious escape amidst the vibrant pulse of the city. Located just 2,8km away from Mouille Point Beach, our hotel embodies the perfect blend of modern elegance and local charm. Sky Hotel was founded on the desire to make happiness, luxury, and indulgence as affordable and accessible as possible. At Sky Hotel, we don't just wish to provide a nice and affordable place to stay. Our vision is to provide every guest and visitor with an amazing and extraordinary experience. From our seamless app-based check-in and room access technology to our stunning design, we have spared no cost in making our hotels as unique and beautiful as possible. But we have not just invested in cutting-edge technology and stunning design; we have also invested in exceptional people. While facilities, technology, amenities, and indulgences are vital, what truly distinguishes Sky Hotel is our fantastic staff of committed and passionate experts. Individuals who embody the Sky Hotel dream. Your dream. Our reality.
